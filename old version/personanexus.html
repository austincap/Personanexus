Personanexus

It's fun to put people into boxes. From horoscopes to 'which Beatle are you?' type quizzes *a certain segment* of the population have always been into this. The ultimate goal of Personanexus is to draw parallels between all the different ways to categorize personalities. Check out the adjacent pic courtesy of Cracked.     This is where anyone can add new groups and personas to build the database. A persona group is the collection of individual personas. For instance, the Meyers-Briggs Type Indicator is the persona group for the 16 included personality types/personas. Duplicate persona groups are periodically merged. Bored?
